4. Total Fixed Cost = − Σ Fixed items
5. Target Net Profit = Sales + Total Cost
6. Contribution Margin = Sales + Product + Variable
7. CM % = CM ÷ Sales
8. Break-Even Sales = − Total Fixed Cost ÷ CM %
