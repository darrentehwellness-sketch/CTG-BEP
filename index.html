②Ad Channels & ROASMeta · ManyChat · +

Revenue = Orders × AOV · ROAS = Revenue ÷ Spend · CPA = Spend ÷ Orders
No Channel Ad Spend Orders AOV Revenue ROAS CPA Net (after Ad)
Totals: — — — — — — —
📖 Show calculation logic
1. Contribution per unit = Selling Price × (1 − Payment% − Platform%) − COGS − Packaging − Shipping
2. Blended Margin % = Σ(Contribution × Units) ÷ Σ(Revenue)
3. Break-Even ROAS = 1 ÷ Margin %  (e.g. 40% margin → 2.5× BE ROAS)
4. Channel ROAS = (Orders × AOV) ÷ Ad Spend
5. Channel Net = (Revenue × Margin %) − Ad Spend
6. Verdict: Profitable if Blended ROAS > Break-Even ROAS
