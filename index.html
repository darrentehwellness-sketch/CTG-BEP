Totals: — — — — —
Margin flag bands: ● Below 70% ● 70 – 74.99% ● 75% and above
📖 Show calculation logic
1. Landed Unit Cost = Package SKU Cost + (SKU × Duties%) + Inbound Freight + Warehousing + Packaging
2. Net Revenue/Unit = Selling Price × (1 − Combined Fee%)
3. Contribution/Unit = Net Revenue/Unit − Landed Unit Cost
4. Margin % = Contribution/Unit ÷ Selling Price
5. Suggested Price = Landed Unit Cost ÷ ((1 − Combined Fee%) − Benchmark%)
6. Safe Price (75%) = Landed Unit Cost ÷ ((1 − Combined Fee%) − 0.75)
7. Flag: 0–69.9% Red · 70–74.99% Amber · 75–100% Green
